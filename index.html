ABOUT ME

THE DRIVER

Welcome to my personal website, where I will introduce you to more about my karting world.

Hello. My name is Theo Ganadik, I am 13 years old and I come from Poprad, Slovakia. I drive for the KidiX driver performance team. I have been a professional karting racer since I was eight years old. I want to be the best, so I go to race and train in Italy where the best of the best are racing. I became a multiple vice-champion of Slovakia, champion of Slovakia in 2024, in the Italian Cup in 2025 I finished in 8th place, even with some bad luck, and in the world championships I finished in the top 10.

You can see more about my journey, training and racing here and on Instagram or Facebook.
